Chocolate Chip Cookies

Mom L's Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are the best cookies ever!

These cookies are packed with chocolate goodness. Do yourself a favor and bake a batch right now.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 12 oz. package Nestl√©'s semi-sweet morsels
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix dry ingredients, set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs.
  5. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls into ungreased baking sheet.
  7. Bake 8-10 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
